Company History

WanderWise started with a simple idea—to make travel easier and more personalized. Founded by a group of travel lovers in 2023, we’ve grown from a small project to a trusted platform helping users discover amazing destinations around the world. Our journey has just begun!

Our Location

WanderWise is proudly based in India, with our main office located in the vibrant city of Chandigarh. Surrounded by creativity and culture, our team works with passion to deliver the best travel recommendations to users across the country and beyond.
